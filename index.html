Our Services

Professional grooming services delivered right to your doorstep. Every pet receives personalized, one-on-one attention.

Full Groom

Complete grooming package including bath, haircut, nail trim, ear cleaning, and finishing touches. Your pet will look and feel their absolute best.

Bath Only

A thorough, refreshing bath with premium shampoo and conditioner, blow dry, and brush out. Perfect for keeping your pet clean between full grooms.

Nail Trim

Quick and careful nail trimming to keep your pet comfortable and their paws healthy. Includes nail filing for smooth edges.

Ear Cleaning

Gentle, thorough ear cleaning to prevent infections and keep your pet comfortable. Essential for breeds prone to ear issues.

Specialty Cut

Breed-specific styling and custom cuts tailored to your pet's breed standards or your personal preferences. Show-quality results.
